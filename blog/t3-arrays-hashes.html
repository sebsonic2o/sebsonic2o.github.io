Ruby Lists
Or thinking about Arrays and Hashes...

Once upon a time, there was a rich nation at peace with all its neighbors. In the North, its magnificent capital city was bordered by its most trusted ally. However, other neighbors were very envious. But only unique properties of the nation's land prevented them from being at war:

To the East, a vast expanse from the city that was easily guarded by the nation,
#land { padding-right: 100px; }

To the South, a natural border that was made up of a large river,
#land { border-bottom: 5px solid blue; }

To the West, a neutral zone expanding away from the border that was not to be crossed,
#land { margin-left: 50px; }

All was well until the evil neighbor from the West discovered that neutral zones could be turned around, and war was on!
#evil { margin-right: -100px; }

Please feel free to use the technical glossary below and read again:
Padding for vast expanse
Border for natural border
Margin for neutral zone
HTML element for nation, neighbor, or land
Content for city
North for top
East for right
South for bottom
West for left
